Austin Long is an Art Director and Designer at Square.

Experience

For over 10 years, I’ve worked both in-house and independently to design meaningful, engaging experiences on the internet and offline. I‘ve led creative strategy for international marketing campaigns, designed digital products, and led multidisciplinary creative teams. I’ve also designed and built websites, brands, merch and illustrations for friends, neighborhood businesses and local organizations.

Process

On every project, I prioritize group cohesion, gradual stiffening and field theory as essential ingredients for developing high-quality creative work. Here’s what I mean by that:

  1. Make sure your team gets along
  2. Keep your process flexible
  3. Consider the full context

From my experience, sticking to these principles increases the chance that the work you produce will resonate with audiences of varying intentions, interests and attention spans. It also makes projects more fun and interesting for everyone involved.
