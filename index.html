For over 10 years, I’ve worked both in-house and independently to design meaningful, engaging experiences on the internet and offline.

Experience

I‘ve led creative strategy for international marketing campaigns, designed digital products, and led multidisciplinary creative teams. I’ve also designed and built websites, brands, merch and illustrations for friends, neighborhood businesses and local organizations.

Principles

On every project, I prioritize group cohesion, gradual stiffening and field theory as essential ingredients for developing high-quality creative work. Here’s what I mean by that:

  1. Make sure your team gets along
  2. Keep your process flexible
  3. Consider the full context
