Austin Long is an Art Director & Designer at Square.

Over the last 10+ years, I’ve worked with companies both in-house and independently to design purposeful, engaging experiences on the internet and offline.

Experience

Since entering the creative workforce in 2011, I’ve held titles like Communications Designer, Interactive Designer, Design Manager and Art Director. I‘ve led creative strategy for international marketing campaigns, designed digital products, and led multidisciplinary creative teams. I’ve also designed and built websites, brands, merch and illustrations for friends, neighborhood businesses and local organizations.

Principles

On every project, I prioritize group cohesion, gradual stiffening and field theory as essential ingredients for developing high-quality creative work. Here’s what I mean by that:

  1. Make sure your team gets along
  2. Keep your process flexible
  3. Consider the full context
